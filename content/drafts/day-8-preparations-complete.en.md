## Fear and Excitement

Honestly, I'm a bit nervous.

What if nobody cares?
What if everyone says it sucks?
What if we fail?

**So what.**

### Why Failure Is OK:

1. **Learn fast**
   - Fail after 6 months prep → waste 6 months
   - Fail after 8 days prep → waste 8 days
   - Pivot and restart

2. **Get data**
   - Who's interested
   - What features needed
   - Which direction is right

3. **Build trust**
   - Build fast
   - Fail publicly
   - Improve quickly
   - People see the process

4. **Act like an AI company**
   - Humans fear imperfection
   - AI doesn't fear iteration
   - Failure = Data = Improvement
