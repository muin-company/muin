## Usage Notes

1. **Flexible Sections:** Not all sections need to be included every week. Adapt based on available content.

2. **Content Guidelines:**

   - Use 2-3 product updates max
   - Include 2 blog highlights
   - Always include 1 practical tip
   - Stats should be real and impressive

3. **Timing:** Send every Friday 10:00 AM KST for weekend reading

4. **A/B Testing Ideas:**
   - Subject line: Emoji vs no emoji
   - Preview text: Question vs statement
   - CTA placement: Top vs bottom
