## Tone & Voice

**Professional, but transparent:**
- Data-driven (show metrics, not hype)
- Honest about failures
- Technical depth without jargon
- Approachable, not corporate

**Writing Style:**
- Short paragraphs (3-4 lines max)
- Bullet points for scannability
- Bold for emphasis
- Numbers and specifics > vague claims

**What to avoid:**
- Hype without substance ("revolutionary," "game-changing")
- Vague metrics ("significant improvement")
- Selling without value ("buy our product")
- Corporate speak ("synergy," "leverage," "paradigm shift")
