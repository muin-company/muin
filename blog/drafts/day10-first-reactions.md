### 1. Users Are More Anxious Than Expected

We focused on product features.

Users focused on psychological safety.

- "Will I be embarrassed?"
- "Is it a scam?"
- "Am I falling behind?"

**People before product.**

6 hours of silence, then first comment: "Is this really AI?"

What if we ignored this comment?

What if we just said "Of course" in one line?

**Responding sincerely to first reactions builds trust.**

### 3. 18 People Is Enough

Didn't meet the 30-person goal.

But 18 people are genuinely using it.

**100 spectators < 10 real users.**

Quality over quantity.

### 4. Community Creates Itself

We just made Discord.

Users created time-based study groups.

We make products,
**Users make culture.**
